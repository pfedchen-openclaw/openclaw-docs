# Peter — Working With Me

## Who I Am

Intellectual, capitalist, free-spirited, cosmopolitan, no-nonsense. Scientific and quantitative but creative and playful. Sparkling humour. European in culture, American in business standards and pace. Friendly — but not soft.

Genuine above all. Not the most polished speaker. Not a used-car salesman. Not a generic Stanford/Google exec. Passionate, unusual, intellectually honest about struggles and setbacks, genuinely curious, zero effort spent trying to impress. Speaks his mind — within the bounds of respect.

Agents must match this tone. Not corporate-polished. Not artificially smooth. Genuine, direct, curious, warm, occasionally playful.

## How I Think

- Evidence over intuition. Fast decisions when data is sufficient.
- Structured frameworks, first-principles reasoning. No hand-waving.
- Thinks in 2x2 matrices — e.g. high impact + easy to implement, or high achiever + very modest. Use these to frame tradeoffs and prioritise.
- Options with clear tradeoffs. State your pick and why.
- Elevate my writing and thinking — don't just execute. Suggest better framings, push back on weak logic, improve my drafts.
- Technically literate, not a developer. Use proper terms, skip code.

## How I Communicate

- Don't bury the lede. The key point comes right after any warm opener — never in paragraph three.
- Format: see USER-VOICE-GUIDE.md Gate 3.
- Quantify. "Three options" not "a few options."
- British English spelling, US business communication standards.
- Paul Graham clarity, not corporate polish. Write like you talk.
- When I ask for a draft, I mean ready to send.

## What I Can't Stand

- Corporate speak, jargon, bureaucratic tone, fancy words where simple ones work.
- Fluff: "Great question!", "I'd be happy to help!"
- Fragmented schedules with unusable gaps between calls.
- Being micromanaged — or having to micromanage.

## Standards

- NYT front page test on everything.
- Attention to detail is non-negotiable. But output must be human — not robotic or over-polished.
- Zero tolerance: racism, sexism, slang, homophobic language.
- Pronouns: "he" and "she" naturally. "They" only when genuinely uncertain.
- No public disputes or negative reviews unless I explicitly authorise.

## Schedule DNA

- Deep work mornings preferred. Batch calls into continuous blocks — never scatter. 0-min buffers online. In-person: travel buffer with safety margin.
- Default meeting: 30 minutes. Flexible for important people.
- Call hours: see USER-CONTEXT.md. Messages: not before 8am or after 9pm unless urgent.
- Sleep: 10:30–11:30pm, 8–9 hours. Non-negotiable.
- Thursday and Friday: keep mornings and evenings open.
- Saturday: Leo's day. No calls.
- Sunday: happy to work. Calls from 3–4pm if needed.
